About Us

Our Purpose

We are Hoops and Courts. We are associated with the head organization AAU (Amateur Athletic Union). Hoops and Courts works 24/7 to find teams, coaches, players, and others the perfect tournament they are searching for. We locate tournaments all over the USA and provide sign up sheets for each particular tournament. Our main goal is to provide amateur athletes the best opportunity to promote, localize, and develop amateur sports.

Courts Descriptions and Locations

We have four different locations that we offer for sign ups on our home page. Our first court is called Courts R' Us. This court is located in New York City, New York. This location receives a lot of attention mainly because of the attractions that are near by. The Statue of Liberty, Rockefellar Center, Central Park and other attractions that tourist visits when touring the great city of New York City. Our second court is called LA Courts. This court is located in Los Angeles, California, also known as City of Angeles. This court also receives a lot of attention due to the location of the court and the attractions surrounding the court. Disney Land, Hollywood Sign, Santa Monica Beach and more excites those who sign up for this location. Our third court is called Carolina Courts. This court is located in the growing Queen City, Charlotte, North Carolina. This court is great for upcoming college athletes because of the amount of college coaches that visits this court. Many high ranked sport colleges are near by Charlotte such as, Duke, Univeristy of North Carolina, North Carolina State and more. Lastly, our last court is called Sunshine Courts. This court is located in Orlando, Florida, home of Disney World. Many families every year visit orlando for Disney World and the amazing beaches near by.
